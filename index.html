Why Should You One Bag?

Lost your luggage before? Ever had the opportunity for a free flight but couldn't switch because of your bags? Well all of your worries could go away by commiting to One Bag. Essentially, you are only carrying The essentials with you. You no longer have to worry if you have all 10 of your bags. It opens up doors for you, almost literally

What Bag Should You Get?

There are lots of bags to choose and ultimately will be up to you. The best thing to remeber is to not get anything more than 45L (or if you go to a lot of small European countries, 35L). You'll want something durable and won't stick out (Tortuga Outbreaker or the Minaal are some) Good Choices

What Chothes You Get?

Stay simple. When traveling to foreign countries, you don't want to stand out too much (pickpocketers and all). You need about four or five shirts...max! Two pairs of pants and maybe a pair of shorts if it'll be a warm place. Most places yo can easily wash your clothes. Think of how you might dress on weekend, use that philosophy!

What Shoes should You Get?

Comfy shoes. You'll be going through airports a lot, so you'll need make sure that whatever you get is easy to take off (Trust me on this one) Make sure they also fit the activities you'll be doing. If you are going to hike some trails...then get shoes for that

Other Essentials

Make sure of course you have a passport, towel, and other items
